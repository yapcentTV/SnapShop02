Feedback

Information provided by customers about their experience with a product or service. The opinions, comments, feedback, and reviews customers share about their online shopping experiences with brands.

Exchange

Online currency exchanges make money by charging a nominal fee and/or through the bid-ask spread in a currency. The exchange policy can be considered a fundamental part of the sales process.

Package

Products that store and protect items bought online until they reach the customer as wells as designing and creating packaging for a product. Makes it easy to transport, and can be used to promote it.

Tracking

A process used by eCommerce businesses to track the progress of customer orders. It typically involves offering a tracking portal, providing a unique tracking number, showing the estimated delivery date, and reflecting timely eCommerce order status.

Return & Refund

Refund means a refund from the seller to the buyer, while the return is the return of goods from the buyer to the seller. The object returned for the refund is in the form of money, while the object returned is in the form of goods.

Security

The guideline that ensures safe transactions through the internet. It consists of protocols that safeguard people who engage in online selling and buying goods and services. You need to gain your customers' trust by putting in place eCommerce security basics.
